FAQ Q&A
1
What is Peckers?
Ans
Peckers is a premium Quick-service restaurant based in the United Kingdom, specialising in freshly prepared Fried and Grilled chicken. We are dedicated to serving the best chicken in Hertfordshire by focusing on high-quality ingredients, bold flavours, and fast, friendly service. Whether you are looking for a quick takeaway or a hot delivery to your door, we pride ourselves on providing Seriously Good Chicken every time.
2
Is Peckers a chain?
Ans
We are a growing independent brand! Currently, we have two flagship sites in Hitchin and Stevenage, and we are excited to be expanding further across the region. Even as we grow, we remain a community-based business at heart. Our focus is always on putting our customers first and ensuring every neighbourhood we join gets the same seriously good chicken and friendly service we are known for.

What makes Peckers different from other chicken shops?
Ans
Peckers stands out by combining the speed of a quick-service restaurant (QSR) with the gourmet taste and quality you’d expect from premium chicken brands. We focus on bold flavours, high-quality ingredients, and our signature housemade sauces that you won't find anywhere else. Most importantly, we are obsessed with customer service ensuring every guest gets a friendly, top-tier experience whether they are dining in, taking away, or ordering delivery.

What do you serve at Peckers?

We are specialists in both fried and grilled chicken. Our signature fried chicken is made using our own unique, secret-recipe breading for the perfect crunch every time. We also offer a premium PERI-PERI grilled chicken range for those looking for a flame-grilled alternative. To round off your meal, don't miss our special milkshakes, thick, creamy, and the perfect partner to our seriously good chicken.

Ans

Is the food at Peckers fresh?
Ans
Absolutely. To ensure our chicken never goes dry, we cook in small fresh batches throughout the day. Whether you walk through our doors at lunch or late at night, you are promised Seriously Good Chicken that is fresh, succulent, and cooked to order. By using industry-leading Henny Penny pressure fryers, we lock in the moisture of every tender and fillet, delivering a perfect crunch and juicy finish across all our stores every single time.
